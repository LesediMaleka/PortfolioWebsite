Skills and Passions

I am a devoted coder at heart. I acquired skills in the following coding languages: HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I am proficient at using design platforms such as Gimp, InkScape and Canva to design wireframes and manipulate images for all web development projects. I am a zealous creative. I am a neophyte photographer, with the ability to capture storytelling and unique timeless moments.

I am multilingual: the languages I am fluent in are English, Sesotho, Sestwana and Afrikaans. I love the outdoors and watching sports (mainly soccer and cricket). I have excellent interpersonal skills , I am: able to work well with others, an active listener, a critical thinker, a problem solver, responsible and reliable. I am able to establish successful relationships among diverse groups. I handle pressurised situations well. In demanding environments I am able to adapt and multi-task whenever new challenges are given to me.

My future goals

I would love to have the opportunity to develop others. It would be an honour if I could help shape the minds of young people through programming. I would like to be known and remembered as an influencer within the programming fraternity. It is my goal to use my skills, such as front-end and (hopefully someday) back-end web development, as well as video games to draw the interest of the youngsters, into becoming future programmers.

I'm hoping to be a real shaper by using the knowledge I have obtained from ADA to produce the next generation of coders. It is important to expose children to programming at an early age as I believe that in the foundation phase it is so essential to learn computer programming.

Nutrition is my other interest. I would like to influence policymakers in ensuring that children receive good, wholesome nutrition. The value of nutrition is key to the overall physical and mental health state of a person. I do this in my life and have enjoyed the benefits of it, and it is so beneficial.
